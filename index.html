Introduction

I know, I know, this website doesn't look that good. And you know why? It's hard! I thought it would be difficult, but like many of my projects, I underestimated its rigor. But I guess that's what makes programming so fun! I started working on this website in the beginning of December 2016. It took me a lot of tutorials and quite a bit of trial and error to wrap my head around how web programming (if you can call it that, it's really just a bunch of HTML and CSS) works. I'll get into Javascript and other libraries a bit later.

The reason why this website looks so bad as of January 3rd, 2016, is because I didn't want to use any auto-generated stuff like weebly or wordpress. I'm not the type of person to borrow off someone else's work if I have no idea what's going on. As a result, I've taken a lot of time to get adjusted to making websites, but I also have a larger amount of control over what I can do. MAIN POINT: This website is built from scratch.

So bear with me, this is going to take me a little while, but I'll eventually make this into something decent.

QUICK NOTE

I'm the type of person who likes to do everything himself. And I find more joy in the actual process of creating something than seeing something complete, so I have lots of unfinished projects. This may even become one.

So take a look around, explore this website that doesn't look too good right now. If you come back in a week or so, it will probably have changed. The longer you wait the more it will have changed (hopefully)!
